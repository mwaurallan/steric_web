About Us

We are a registered company in Kenya, situated in Limuru town in the tea growing zone of the formerly White highlands. The climate is ideal for the growth of high quality tea green leaf which is globally recognized as a unique beverage.

The company strives to satisfy its customers both locally and internationally with first class teas as per the customer’s requirements..

Tea is regarded as the most famous non-alcoholic drink in the world. Records show that the history of drinking tea can be dated back to two thousand years ago, which last until today. What’s more, the consumption of tea presents a trend of slow increase these days.

With the increase of people’s health sense and the verifying of the medical value of tea contents, in future tea will continue to perform an important role in the international drinks market. Kenya is the hometown of tea and occupies an important position in the world market.

Our Products

Our main products includes tea sachets of 500gms, 250gms,100gms,and 50gms which are distributed through local outlets. Many of the local people while travelling out of the country have been marketing our teas due to the high quality products from our company..

We supply various tea drinking outlets in Kenya with our teas and this line has been very popular especially with tourists who have had a true taste of the Kenya Tea.

We also deal in bulk teas locally and abroad as per our client’s requirements. Various grades are in offer such as PF1, PD, BP1,BP,PF, Dust and Fannings.
